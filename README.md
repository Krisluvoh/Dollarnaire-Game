# Dollarnaire-Game
The Objective:

To see which of two players will reach a minimum of a $1 first in random coin increments (1 cent, 5 cents, 10 cents and 25 cents) with alternate pushes of a button.

The Rules:

Each player will alternate turns pushing a button. When the button is pushed, they will get a random coin amount of 1 cent, 5 cents, 10 cents or 25 cents. Players will alternate turns until one of them accumulates coins totaling a minimum of a $1. The first player to accumulate coins totaling a minimum of $1 is declared the winner.
